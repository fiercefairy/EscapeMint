# Release v0.4.0 - Platform-Level Cash Tracking

Released: 2025-12-15

## Overview

This release introduces platform-level cash tracking with automatic cash fund creation, separating trading activity from cash management for clearer financial tracking.

## 🎉 New Features

### Platform-Level Cash Tracking
- **Auto-Create Cash Funds**: Automatically creates dedicated cash funds for each platform
- **Fund Type System**: Introduced `FundType` enum with `'trading'` and `'cash'` types
- **Cash Isolation**: Trading funds now have `manage_cash=false` by default
- **Aggregate Metrics**: Dashboard shows combined cash balance across all platforms

### Dashboard Improvements
- **Cash Fund Pinning**: Cash funds appear at the top of fund lists for easy access
- **Aggregate Panel**: New panel showing total fund size, current value, and cash balance
- **Time-Weighted Fund Size (TWFS)**: More accurate aggregate metrics using time-weighted calculations

## 🔧 Improvements

- Cleaner separation between invested capital and available cash
- Better tracking of platform-wide liquidity
- Foundation for multi-account management
