# Release v0.12.0 - Philosophy Documentation & Harvest Mode Rename

Released: 2026-01-11

## Overview

This release adds comprehensive documentation explaining the "why" behind EscapeMint - its retirement-focused philosophy and long-only investment approach. It also renames "Liquidate Mode" to "Harvest Mode" throughout the application to better reflect the positive nature of profit extraction.

## 🎉 New Features

### Philosophy Documentation
- **New Philosophy Guide**: `docs/philosophy.md` - comprehensive guide explaining why EscapeMint exists
  - Retirement system philosophy (not day trading)
  - Long-only, forever-asset investment approach
  - Fund lifecycle: Seed → Grow → Liftoff → Money Tree
  - Accumulate vs Harvest mode strategies
  - M1 Finance borrow strategy for tax-efficient retirement income
  - Asset selection criteria (indexes, BTC, leveraged ETFs)

### Visual Diagrams (SVG)
- **Fund Lifecycle Diagram**: `docs/diagrams/fund-lifecycle.svg` - visual showing the 4 phases from seed to money tree
- **DCA Tiers Flowchart**: `docs/diagrams/dca-tiers.svg` - decision flowchart for buy/sell tier selection
- **Accumulate vs Harvest**: `docs/diagrams/accumulate-vs-harvest.svg` - side-by-side comparison of the two modes

### Why "Harvest" Instead of "Liquidate"?

The term "liquidate" has negative connotations (forced selling, bankruptcy). "Harvest" better reflects the positive nature of this mode:
- Harvesting profits from a mature position
- Like harvesting crops from a garden you've tended
- Aligned with the "money tree" metaphor in the philosophy
